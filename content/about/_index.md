This is a blog about software engineering, security, and the craft of building things that last. It is written by people who care more about getting things right than getting things first.

## What you'll find here

**Essays** are long-form pieces — typically 1,500 to 3,000 words — exploring a single idea in depth. They are published when they are ready, not on a schedule.

**Notes** are shorter observations, technical references, and links worth preserving. They are less polished and more frequent.

## Who writes this

The authors are working engineers. They build systems, debug production incidents, and read more RFCs than blog posts. They believe that clear writing and clear thinking are the same discipline.

## Contact

You can reach us by email or find us on GitHub. We read everything and reply when we can.
